Questions, answered.

How does watermark removal work?
We use the Gemini watermark catalog to detect the logo on every frame, then apply mathematical reverse alpha blending — the exact inverse of how the watermark was originally added. The output is pixel-identical to what your video looked like before watermarking.
Where do my files go?
Your video is uploaded to our server, processed, and immediately deleted. We never store, log, analyze, or share your content. Files auto-expire within 24 hours even if you forget to download.
What formats and sizes are supported?
Input: MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI up to 500MB. Output preserves your input format and codec — no re-encoding, no quality loss.
How long does processing take?
Usually 30 seconds to 3 minutes depending on video length, resolution, and current server load. You'll see real-time progress.
Do I need to sign up?
No signup for the free web tool — just upload and process. API access requires a free account with API key.
Can I use the cleaned video commercially?
We don't claim any rights over your files. You retain full ownership. Please review Google's Veo terms for usage rights on the underlying generated content.
